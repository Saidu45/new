Assignment: Health and Nutrition

Below are the answers to your assignment questions:

1. List ten foods you should avoid when you are suffering from obesity and give reasons why five of them should be avoided:

Foods to avoid:

  • Sugary drinks (e.g., soda)
  • Fast foods (e.g., burgers, fries)
  • White bread
  • Pastries and cakes
  • Ice cream
  • Fried foods
  • Processed meats (e.g., sausages)
  • Candy and chocolates
  • Pizza with extra cheese
  • Alcoholic beverages

Reasons to avoid five of these foods:

  • Sugary drinks: High in empty calories and sugar, contributing to weight gain and insulin resistance.
  • Fast foods: Often high in trans fats and calories, leading to increased body fat and heart disease risk.
  • White bread: Lacks fiber and has a high glycemic index, causing rapid blood sugar spikes and hunger.
  • Fried foods: High in unhealthy fats and calories, promoting inflammation and obesity.
  • Processed meats: Contain high levels of sodium and saturated fats, linked to obesity and cardiovascular issues.
